## 🔥 What I'm Up To
I'm focused on building reliable DevOps pipelines, automating infrastructure, and improving observability. I love working at the intersection of development and operations to deliver fast, stable, and secure software.
  
## 🚀 What I’m Learning & Building
Right now, I'm sharpening my expertise in:

* CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, Jenkins)
* Infrastructure as Code (Terraform)
* Containers & Orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)
* Cloud platforms (AWS)
* Monitoring & Observability (Prometheus, Grafana)
* Linux administration & scripting (Bash, Python)

## 💡 Why DevOps?
I’m passionate about enabling teams to move faster with confidence. DevOps lets me:

* `Automate deployments`
* `Scale systems reliably`
* `Improve uptime and performance`
* `Create secure, repeatable workflows`
